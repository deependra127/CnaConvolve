About Convolve
Convolve is a Global innovation challenge where IIT teams compete to solve real-world problems. It brings industry-backed cases, expert mentorship, and a platform to showcase cutting-edge solutions.
4500+Participants
Engaging students from top IITs and technical institutes across Globe.
40+Institutions across globe
Competing in ML, AI, and Quant challenges across multiple rounds.
A Platform built for Impact

For Participants

Compete in India's Premier Pan-IIT AI/ML Hackathon covering multiple tracks.

  • Work on high-quality, industry-backed challenges
  • Get mentored by experts & researchers
  • Offline finales at IIT Guwahati, IIT Madras & IIT Kanpur
  • Win certificates, cash prizes & potential internship opportunities

For Organisations

Convolve gives companies direct access to top talent from across IITs, along with Global visibility.

  • Engage with high-quality student talent
  • Launch custom problem statements
  • Mentor finalists across three IIT campuses
  • Gain strong digital branding & wide reach
